Frequently Asked Questions

How long should a hug last for health benefits?
Research by Dr. Jan Astrom suggests hugs lasting 20 seconds or more trigger the release of oxytocin, the bonding hormone. This can reduce stress, lower blood pressure, and improve mood. Even a 10-second hug can have measurable benefits.
What is a bear hug?
A bear hug is a very firm, full-body embrace where both arms wrap tightly around the other person, often lifting them slightly. It conveys deep affection, warmth, and security. The name comes from how bears are perceived to embrace their cubs.
How many hugs do we need per day?
Family therapist Virginia Satir famously said: "We need 4 hugs a day for survival. We need 8 hugs a day for maintenance. We need 12 hugs a day for growth." Most people fall well short of this — which is exactly why this app exists.
Can I use this for a friendly hug, not just romantic?
Absolutely. Hugs are for everyone — partners, friends, family, pets. Select "Friend Hug" or "Quick Hug" mode for a platonic embrace. All hug types are welcome here.
